Loci.Garden · Sovereignty Roadmap

The knowledge layer
for the sovereignty stack.

Loci is an AI-native knowledge garden, your second brain, privately held. This roadmap is our public commitment to the protocols, tools, and communities that share our values.

Your mind is the last private space. They call it an assistant. It is a recording device.

You think inside their systems, you train them. You remember inside their products, you populate them. Your cognitive labour is the input. Their leverage is the output.

We are not building that.

Cognitive Sovereignty Manifesto · Loci.Garden · 2026

Tier 1

Q3 2026, Ship It
1A
Ollama Local Inference
Loci uses any open-weight model (Llama, Mistral, Gemma) as its AI engine. nomic-embed-text for local semantic indexing. Zero API keys, zero cloud, zero telemetry. If Ollama goes offline, Loci fails closed, never silently calls external APIs.
Kata Ollama · nomic-embed Local inference
Shipped
1B
MCP Plugin, Goose & Beyond
Loci publishes an open-source MCP server. Any MCP-compatible agent (Goose, Continue.dev, Claude Code) can query your knowledge garden as context. Locus nodes and Rooms are exposed as MCP resources. This is Loci's first external API, built sovereign-first.
Cipher MCP · Goose · Apache 2.0 THREAT-01 gate
Shipped
1C
Nostr Keypair Identity
Sign into Loci with your Nostr keypair via NIP-07 browser extension (Alby, nos2x). The garden is yours before the first note is written. Your private key never touches Loci's code. Since there's no existing auth model, this is Loci's first, sovereign by default.
Cipher Nostr · NIP-07 · secp256k1
In progress
1D
Nym Partnership Announcement
Formal alignment between Loci and Nym Technologies. Loci commits to metadata-private sync (Q4). Nym signals Loci as an aligned ecosystem partner. Loci protects the content of your thinking; Nym protects the fact that you're thinking. Together, they close a gap neither can close alone.
Rune Nym · Mixnet · Partnership Requires 1A + 1B live
Not started

Nym Private Sync
Route Loci sync traffic through the Nym mixnet. Metadata-invisible, no observer, including Nym's relays, can see what you're syncing or when. Fail-closed: if Nym routing fails, sync pauses. Never a silent fallback to unprotected state.
